## Now

🌱 &nbsp;**Led all the technology at AgroUrbana** (4+ years). Started as IT Projects Engineer and grew to run the whole tech of the biggest vertical farm in LATAM, from the climate algorithm to the PCB that runs it. 400,000+ plants, 99.8% uptime, ~70M data points/month.

🔌 &nbsp;**Hardware**: 14 industrial PCB designs and 200+ sensing & control units in the field, at roughly 1/10th the cost of off-the-shelf gear. The open-source one is **QiU**.

🤖 &nbsp;**AI**: built the farm's AI layer (climate-analysis apps, smart control algorithms, multi-agent systems). Early Claude adopter, 3 years in. Also documented and cleaned up 25+ repos with Claude Code.

🏗️ &nbsp;**Self-taught builder**: Constructor Civil (UC Chile) who taught himself electronics, PCB design, software, IoT and AI. Ex-founder of **NuLight (IvY)**, AI lighting for human wellbeing, and **Smart Home Chile**.

🍄 &nbsp;**On the side**: growing and extracting medicinal mushrooms at [mushfarms.cl](https://mushfarms.cl).

💬 &nbsp;**Open to work**: automation, AI agents, IoT/AgTech, hardware and data. Remote-first.
